Overview

Purpose

As the proud owner of Dry Oar WhiteWater Rafting, I recognize the need to establish an online presence for our white water rafting business. The purpose of our website is multi-faceted, aiming to:

  • Build Awareness: Introduce our rafting adventures to a broader audience, increasing brand visibility.
  • Attract Customers: Showcase the thrill and excitement of our rafting trips, enticing adventure enthusiasts to experience the adrenaline rush.
  • Provide Information: Offer comprehensive details about our rafting packages, safety measures, and other essential information for potential customers.
  • Foster Trust: Establish credibility and trust with our audience by transparently presenting our company values, commitment to safety, and customer testimonials.
  • Facilitate Bookings: Enable seamless booking processes, allowing interested individuals to easily plan and reserve their rafting adventures online.

This website serves as a digital gateway for both adventure seekers and curious individuals, creating a compelling online presence that reflects the spirit and excitement of Dry Oar Rafting Adventures.

Audience

Our target audience for the white water rafting website includes adventure enthusiasts and individuals seeking thrilling outdoor experiences. The primary characteristics of our audience are:

  • Age: 18-40
  • Interest in outdoor activities and adventure sports
  • Financially active for recreational pursuits

Our audience is looking for:

  • Exciting and challenging rafting trips
  • Information on safety measures during rafting
  • Details about our rafting packages and services

They expect to find a user-friendly website that provides easy navigation on various devices, including desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.
